About Televet Health

Our Mission

Welcome to our Veterinary Telehealth System with Image-Based Dog and Cat Skin Disease Identification. This platform was developed to bridge the gap between pet owners and veterinary care by combining modern telehealth services with advanced image-based disease detection technology.

How It Works

Our system utilizes the EfficientNet-B0 deep learning model to assist in identifying common skin diseases in dogs and cats through uploaded images. By integrating artificial intelligence with veterinary teleconsultation, the platform aims to provide a convenient, accessible, and time-efficient solution for early detection and professional guidance.

Our Team

We are a team of researchers and developers dedicated to improving pet healthcare through technology. This system was designed to support veterinarians in their diagnostic process and empower pet owners with preliminary insights into their pets' conditions.

🛡️ Disclaimer

While the system provides helpful identification results, it is intended to assist and not replace professional veterinary diagnosis. Our goal is to promote better decision-making and timely medical attention for pets.
